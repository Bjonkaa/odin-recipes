Porridge

The best porridge recipe- Perfect porrige for a healthy way to start the day!

Ingredients

  • 50g porridgeoats
  • 350ml milk, water or a mixture of the two
  • a pinch of salt
  • Greek yogurt and honey for topping

Steps

  1. Put 50g porridge oats in a saucepan, pour in 350ml milk or water and sprinkle in a pinch of salt.
  2. Bring to the boil and simmer for 4-5 minutes, stirring from time to time and watching carefully that it doesnâ€™t stick to the bottom of the pan.
  3. Or you can try this in a microwave. Mix the porridge oats, milk or water and a pinch of salt in a large microwaveproof bowl, then microwave on High for 5 minutes, stirring halfway through. Leave to stand for 2 minutes before eating.
  4. To serve, pour into bowls, spoon Greek yogurt, thinned with a little milk, on top and drizzle with honey.
